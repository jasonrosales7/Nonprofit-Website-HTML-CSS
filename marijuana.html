Marijuana

What is Marijuana?

Marijuana can be consumed in flower or concentrate form, as well as by eating or drinking it. The main element from marijuana that makes the user high is a chemical compount called THC. There are many current studies on the effects of THC on the developing and developed brain, but some currently known side effects of marijuana include altered senses, changes in mood,impaired body movement, and difficulty solving problems.None have died from marijuana usage, but there are some serious health effects associated with marijuana consumption such as breathing issues and anxiety.Marijuana has many slang names, such as weed,pot,bud, and broccoli. While much is still unknown about marijuana, there is enough research and evidence to show how and where marijuana can be harmful. What is Vaping?

Vaping is the act of inhaling a vapor made from a liquid or dry material that is heated in an electronic device called an e cigarette. These e-cigarettes or vaporizers come in a variety of shapes and sizes. Due to the rise of vaping, using vaporizers to consume drugs other than nicotine is also on the rise. Now vapes are being used to smoke marijuana in concentrated oil form. This form of marijuana is much more potent and can cause anxiety, paranoia, and hallucinations. Although it is sometimes reported that the smoke is just water vapor, most vaping devices release harmful chemicals and metals into the bloodstream.
